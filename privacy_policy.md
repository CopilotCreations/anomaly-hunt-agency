# Privacy Policy for Dead Pixel Detective

**Last Updated: December 2024**

## Introduction

Dead Pixel Detective ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## Summary

**Dead Pixel Detective is designed with privacy in mind:**
- We do NOT collect any personal data
- The app works 100% offline
- Sensor data is processed locally and never transmitted
- Ads are non-personalized

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Dead Pixel Detective does not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Gameplay data or statistics to external servers
- Photos, contacts, or other personal content
- Browsing history or search queries

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

### Game Progress
- High score
- Highest level reached
- Total anomalies found
- User preferences (accessibility settings)

This data is stored using Android's DataStore and never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data.

### Preferences
- High contrast mode setting
- Reduced motion setting
- Haptic feedback setting
- Sound effects setting
- Show hints setting

## Sensor Data Usage

The app uses the following device sensors **for gameplay mechanics only**:

### Accelerometer
- **Purpose**: Detect device rotation for dynamic anomaly visibility
- **Processing**: Real-time, local processing only
- **Storage**: Not stored
- **Transmission**: Never transmitted

### Rotation Vector Sensor
- **Purpose**: Track device orientation for gameplay mechanics
- **Processing**: Real-time, local processing only
- **Storage**: Not stored
- **Transmission**: Never transmitted

### Screen Brightness
- **Purpose**: Adjust anomaly visibility based on brightness level
- **Processing**: Read-only access via Settings.System
- **Storage**: Not stored
- **Transmission**: Never transmitted

**All sensor data is processed locally in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.**

## Advertising

Dead Pixel Detective displays advertisements through Google AdMob.

### Ad Configuration
- **Ad Type**: Banner and interstitial ads
- **Personalization**: Ads are NOT personalized
- **Data Collection**: We have configured AdMob to not collect personal data
- **Test Ads**: During development, only test ad units are used

### Google's Privacy Policy
Google AdMob may collect anonymous analytics for ad serving purposes. For more information, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### Opting Out
You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings:
1. Go to Settings > Google > Ads
2. Enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

## Children's Privacy

Dead Pixel Detective does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game is designed to be family-friendly and does not contain:
- In-app purchases
- Social features
- Chat functionality
- Links to social networks
- Personal data collection

## Offline Functionality

Dead Pixel Detective is designed to work 100% offline:
- All game logic runs locally on your device
- No internet connection is required to play
- Internet is only used for displaying advertisements (optional)
- The app functions fully without network connectivity

## Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data:
- There are no external servers storing your information
- Your game progress is secured by your device's security measures
- No cloud sync or backup of game data occurs

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob
- **Purpose**: Display advertisements
- **Data Access**: Anonymous ad serving data only
- **Privacy Policy**: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

We do not integrate any other third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

## Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data:
- There is no personal data to access, modify, or delete
- Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored preferences
- No account creation is required

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our app store listing.

## Compliance

This app is designed to comply with:
- Google Play Developer Program Policies

- Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA)
